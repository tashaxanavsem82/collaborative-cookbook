# Collaborative Cookbook

Welcome to Collaborative Cookbook! This is a web-based application that allows users to join together in the kitchen to create and share recipes. Whether you are a novice home cook or a seasoned chef, our platform allows you to:

### Features:
- **Collaborative Recipe Creation**: Invite friends and family to contribute their favorite recipes.
- **User Profiles**: Create a profile to showcase your culinary creations and cooking experiences.
- **Meal Planning**: Plan your meals by using recipes from your cookbook; get suggestions based on dietary preferences.
- **Cooking Tips and Tricks**: Share and learn cooking tips from other users in the community.
- **Curated Collections**: Create themed collections of recipes (e.g., vegan dishes, holiday favorites).

### Getting Started:
1. Create an account or sign in if you already have one.
2. Start adding recipes by filling out ingredients, instructions, and other details.
3. Invite friends to join your cookbook and share their recipes with you!

### Technology Stack:
- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: Node.js with Express
- Database: MongoDB
- Hosting: Heroku
